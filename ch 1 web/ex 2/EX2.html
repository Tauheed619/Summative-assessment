Plot Overview

Stranger Things is a sci-fi horror series set in the 1980s. It begins with the mysterious disappearance of a young boy in a small Indiana town, uncovering a secret government experiment and the existence of a parallel dimension called the Upside Down.

Key Information

  • Genre: Sci-fi, Horror, Drama
  • Created by: The Duffer Brothers
  • Network: Netflix
  • First aired: July 15, 2016

Cast & Creative Team

  • Winona Ryder as Joyce Byers
  • David Harbour as Jim Hopper
  • Millie Bobby Brown as Eleven
  • Creators: Matt and Ross Duffer

Characters

  • Eleven – A girl with psychokinetic abilities
  • Mike Wheeler – A loyal friend searching for Will
  • Jim Hopper – Police chief uncovering the truth
