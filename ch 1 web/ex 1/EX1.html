Chocolate Chip Cookies

This classic chocolate chip cookie recipe is easy to make, with soft centers and crisp edges. Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cream together the butter and sugars until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Gradually blend the dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  6. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto baking sheets.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until golden brown.
  9. Allow to cool on wire racks before serving.
